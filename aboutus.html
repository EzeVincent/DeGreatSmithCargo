About Us

De Great Smith Cargo & Logistics offers fast, reliable ocean freight, haulage, customs clearance & air freight services, ensuring your shipments reach their destination on time.

Our Story

De Great Smith Cargo started with a vision to blend tradition and innovation in logistics. From our modest beginnings, we’ve grown into a trusted global partner, dedicated to seamless freight forwarding, customs clearing, and logistics solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline global trade with exceptional freight forwarding, customs clearing, and logistics services, ensuring timely and efficient delivery for our clients.

Our Vission

We aim to lead the logistics industry with innovative, reliable solutions, setting new standards and helping businesses thrive in a dynamic world.
