Services

De Great Smith Cargo and Logistics provides efficient air and sea freight, customs clearing, freight forwarding, import and export services, transportation solutions, and air ticketing for seamless global and local logistics.


Air Freight

Swift and reliable air freight solutions to ensure your goods reach their destination quickly and safely. We handle everything from documentation to delivery, making international shipping seamless.

Sea Freight

Cost-effective sea freight services for large and heavy shipments. Our sea freight solutions offer dependable transportation across global ports, with comprehensive tracking and handling.

Expert customs clearing services to navigate complex regulations and ensure smooth import and export processes. We handle all paperwork and compliance issues for hassle-free customs clearance.

Freight Forwarding

Comprehensive freight forwarding solutions to manage the entire logistics chain. From coordinating transportation to handling documentation, we streamline your supply chain for efficiency.

Import and Export

End-to-end import and export services tailored to your business needs. We manage all aspects of international trade, ensuring your goods are processed and transported with precision.

Reliable transportation services for moving goods locally and regionally. Our fleet and logistics network ensure timely and safe delivery of your shipments.
